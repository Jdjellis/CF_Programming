# Knee / tendon rehab

> A non-periodised knee and patellar-tendon rehab menu (low-CNS, daily-friendly).
> Used as the supporting **ACCESSORY** when the class already supplies the heavy
> squat stimulus (SKILL.md §3.3, §5) — quad development + tendon health without a
> second competing barbell squat. There's no week-by-week ramp: pick from the
> menu, progress holds/load by feel, and keep it pain-free (≤ 3/10).

## Drills
- Spanish squat (banded) — 3x20–30s isometric holds (tendon health)
- ATG split squat (bodyweight → goblet) — 3x8/leg, controlled full depth
- Zombie front squat — 3x5 (front-rack + quad / upright-torso bias)
- Bulgarian split squat — 3x8/leg
- Tibialis raises — 3x15 (knee tracking / lower-leg balance)
- Slant-board single-leg sit — 3x10/leg (VMO + tendon load)

## Notes
- Isometrics (Spanish squat / wall sit) are the go-to on a sore or RED day —
  keep these, drop the loaded split / zombie squats.
- Full range, slow eccentrics; stop a set if it provokes sharp tendon pain.
