# Squat 12-week block

> A 12-week front/back-squat strength block built to drive the clean stand-up
> (the recovery-limited lift). Percentages are off the relevant squat 1RM and
> **resolve through the load calculator** via the focus template's strength
> pieces — the drills below are the week's menu and cues, not hand-computed
> kilos. Weeks 4, 8 and 12 are lighter (intensification → realisation); deload
> as the block week calls for it.

## Week 1
Cue: Build in; leave 2 reps in the tank. Own the pause before chasing load.
- Back squat — 5x5 @ 70%
- Paused front squat (3s) — 3x3 @ 70%
- Spanish squat (banded) — 3x20s holds (knee/tendon health)

## Week 2
Cue: Same bar speed, a little more load. Vertical torso out of the hole.
- Back squat — 5x4 @ 75%
- Paused front squat (3s) — 4x3 @ 72.5%
- Bulgarian split squat — 3x8/leg

## Week 3
Cue: Top single is a fast, clean rep — not a grind. Tempo work stays strict.
- Back squat — 4x3 @ 80%
- Paused front squat (3s) — 4x3 @ 75%
- Tempo front squat (32X1) — 3x4

## Week 4
Cue: Down week — keep it crisp and submaximal; let the legs come back.
- Back squat — 3x3 @ 70%
- Front squat — 3x3 @ 70%
- Spanish squat (banded) — 3x20s holds

## Week 5
Cue: Reload heavier than week 3. Drive the knees through on the stand-up.
- Back squat — 5x3 @ 80%
- Paused front squat (3s) — 4x2 @ 80%
- Zombie front squat — 3x5 (front-rack + quad bias)

## Week 6
Cue: Heavy triples, full intent. Brace before you unrack.
- Back squat — 4x3 @ 82.5%
- Front squat — 4x3 @ 80%
- Bulgarian split squat — 3x8/leg

## Week 7
Cue: Doubles now — sharpen the top end without missing.
- Back squat — 5x2 @ 85%
- Paused front squat (3s) — 3x2 @ 82.5%
- Tempo front squat (32X1) — 3x3

## Week 8
Cue: Down week — autoregulate; keep one heavy-ish single if it feels fast.
- Back squat — 3x2 @ 75%
- Front squat — 2x2 @ 75%
- Spanish squat (banded) — 3x30s holds

## Week 9
Cue: Heaviest doubles of the block. Smooth out of the bottom.
- Back squat — 4x2 @ 87.5%
- Front squat — 4x2 @ 82.5%
- Zombie front squat — 3x4

## Week 10
Cue: Singles ramp — stop when bar speed drops, never grind a miss.
- Back squat — work to a heavy single @ ~90%, then 2x2 @ 85%
- Paused front squat (3s) — 3x1 @ 85%
- Bulgarian split squat — 2x8/leg

## Week 11
Cue: Express strength — fast singles, full rest, leave it in the tank.
- Back squat — 3x1 @ 92.5%
- Front squat — 3x1 @ 87.5%
- Tempo front squat (32X1) — 2x3

## Week 12
Cue: Realisation/test week — open conservative, jump only on fast reps.
- Back squat — work to a daily-max single (test)
- Front squat — work to a heavy single @ ~90%
- Spanish squat (banded) — 2x20s holds (cooldown)
